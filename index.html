At the age of 5, already settled in Bialystok, I started to play football. I spent the rest of my childhood playing in the streets of Dziesięciny having fun and feeling more and more attracted by this sport that would eventually become my life’s passion. I joined the Jagiellonia Białystok Academy when I was 8. Being there, apart from making very good friends, I enjoyed football every single day and I started my training as a footballer and as a person. I played eight seasons for the club’s academy (2001-2010). I made my debut as a professional player. I keep great memories of my time in the club! While having a 2 years break with the sport putting more effort into learning at secondary school I found joy in Futsal. I spend 4 years representing MOKS Słoneczny Stok Białystok (2012-2015). After that, I had a little episode in the lower Polish Tier with Magnat (2015) and I left my motherland to start a new episode in my life. I spend 6 years in Great Britain loving every single day meeting so many great people in that beautiful country. Meanwhile living near Nottingham I was part of 2 big local clubs Hucknall Town F.C. (2018-2020) and South Normanton Athletic F.C. (2020-2021). Except that I started going to the gym and do boxing. The Worldwide COVID pandemic changed my plans a little bit and I came back home to my family in Poland again. Nowadays I keep enjoying every aspect of this beautiful discipline by playing semi-pro whenever I can. Currently looking for a new job and new club here.
